About Lugumya

Introduction

Lugumya is a Budgeting and Tracking application designed to help individuals and businesses manage their finances effectively. Our platform provides powerful tools and features that simplify the budgeting process, track expenses, and provide valuable insights to improve financial health.

Company Overview

Lugumya is under SwahiliElites, a technology company based in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania. SwahiliElites focuses on solving problems through the application of information technology. With a deep understanding of and computing, we strive to create innovative solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their goals.
